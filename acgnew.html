ADMIT CARD GENERATOR

Option 1: Manual Entry

Step 1: Select the **Manual Entry** tab below.

Step 2: Fill in all the fields according to your **Exam Form** or previous Admit Card.

Step 3: Click on **Generate Admit Card**.

Option 2: Auto-fill from Upload

Step 1: Select the **Auto-fill from Upload** tab below.

Step 2: Upload your **Exam Form/Admit Card** (PDF Recommended).

Step 3: Review and manually correct any auto-filled details.

Step 4: Click on **Generate Admit Card**.

**Disclaimer:** The data extraction feature is experimental. Always verify all auto-filled details, especially the Paper Codes and Names.
